# Code Of Conduct

Dezh welcomes technical review, critique, and contribution. The project is
small, research-oriented, and security-sensitive, so discussion should remain
precise, respectful, and evidence-driven.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct about technical risks and limitations.
- Separate criticism of an idea from criticism of a person.
- Provide reproduction steps for bugs and security claims.
- Respect coordinated disclosure for security reports.
- Keep discussion focused on the project, its architecture, and its evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing technical review.
- Publicly disclosing unresolved security vulnerabilities without coordination.
- Misrepresenting prototype status as production readiness.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or other project
spaces to keep review useful and safe. Serious or repeated violations may lead
to blocked participation.
